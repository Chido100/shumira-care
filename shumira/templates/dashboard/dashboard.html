FOR THE LOVE OF CARE

Our global population is aging.
A vast majority of our aging population prefer to stay in their own homes than move into a care home. Many countries are facing a shortage of caregivers.


CareVR - Our virtual reality care training platform will offer fun and effective training for those with a heart for caring.
Empowering families and individuals by giving them the basic care skills needed to care for their loved ones at home.

Our Services...


Community Lounge

This is a place for cared for individuals and/or their families to connect for support, chat, build relationships, share experiences, and arrange to meet up. We understand the importance of connecting and sharing experiences with other families or individuals going through the same challenges and how valuable this support can be.

Family Carer

As our parents/grandparents become older, or our loved ones face health challenges, most would prefer to remain and be cared for in their homes. Now with a shortage of caregivers in many countries, this is where we as family members come in. Family members will be able to learn basic care skills to look after their relatives with care needs at home. We believe that everyone of us has the potential to be a carer.

Corporate Care

We offer our corporate care as an employee benefit. Many people are having to leave the workforce or are requiring time off due to the pressures and constraints of caring for a loved one at home. We partner and work with companies and government departments that encourage their employees to learn basic care skills through our platform, providing information and resources for them to provide their loved ones with the best care possible.
