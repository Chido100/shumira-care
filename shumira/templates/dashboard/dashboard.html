About Us

It's about family.

Shumira Care was founded 2017 by Tariro and Esther. Their aim was to provide home care in their local community after witnessing how families often feel ill-equipped and overwhelmed when their loved one suddenly needs care at home. It was during this time that they met families who were struggling to find home carers, and unable to provide basic care for their loved one themselves. They witnessed the importnace of person centred, quality care, and the impact it can have on individuals and their loved ones.

More recently, Tariro and Esther had the privilege of coming together with their family to provide 24/7 care for Esther's Grndfather in his home, until he sadly passed away. While this can be a daunting prospect, the fact is that many families will have to face this in the future, and Shumira Care wants to help.

Our mission is to champion quality care, and make training easily accessible to anyone.

Our vision is to see a transformation in the way we approach care.
